# Research Roadmap

1. **Federated Learning** – Integrate privacy-preserving aggregation
   mechanisms to enable collaborative training across distributed
   recycling facilities.
2. **Active Learning** – Introduce automated sample selection to minimise
   labelling effort while continuously improving the detection model.
3. **Physics-Guided Models** – Combine sensor simulation with neural
   networks to improve robustness under challenging lighting and material
   deformation scenarios.
4. **Edge Optimisation** – Explore quantisation-aware training and model
   distillation for deployment on low-power edge accelerators.
5. **Explainability** – Provide saliency and spectral attribution maps to
   facilitate regulatory compliance and operator trust.
